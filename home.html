- How this service works -

Our service allows customers to purchase products without leaving their cars

Our drive-through usually consists of a building with a driveway wrapped around it.

Drivers approach either a first window or a microphone box and place an order

Then they drive around to the other side of the building

Where the order is delivered through a small window and the customer can pay for it

Remember: It's an express lane, not a sit-down experience.
